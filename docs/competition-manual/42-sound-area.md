# 4.2 Sound Area
- Shade tent (1)
- 2 tables
- Sound system preferably with computer laptop, compatible with iPod, USB thumb drives
- Power supply, cords, adapters
- Sticky notes/post-it notes
- Tape
- Pens
- Sharpie / permanent markers
- Stopwatch
